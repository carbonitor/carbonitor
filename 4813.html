Description:

The Sidrap Wind Energy Project Phase 1 is located Mattirotasi and Lainungan Villages, Watang Pulu Sub-district, Sidrap Regency, South Sulawesi Province, Indonesia. The Project will have a total rated capacity of 75 MW. The power generated by the project will be supplied the public electricity grid or PLN grid via an on-site PLN owned sub-station where it will be transformed to 150 kV. The expected net annual power supply to the grid is 253,000 MWh.
