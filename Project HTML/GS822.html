Description: The project will generate electricity of 1.168MW to be exported to the Sumatra Grid by utilizing Batang Rambutan River in West Sumatra. The project consists of barrage, diversion tunnel, fore bay, spillway. pressure pipe, powerhouse, and booster station to generate electricity. The project has a strong involvement from the local stakeholders.
